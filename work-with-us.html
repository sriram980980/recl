Benefits

As a Reclabs Associate, the following benefits * are available.
Insurance Benefits We offer medical, vision, dental, disability, & prescription drug coverage.
Payroll Bi-weekly Payroll
Direct Deposit Employees can choose to have their pay deposited into a personal bank account right on the payroll date.
Holiday Pay We observe six holidays throughout the year: New years day, Labor Day, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day, Depending on your tenure with Reclabs, and your work schedule, you may qualify to receive holiday pay.
Vacation Bonuses Eligible Employees can accumulate vacation bonuses.
